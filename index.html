Our Story & Values

At DP ENERGIES, we bring over 25 years of combined experience in the gas and energy industry, offering expert solutions for domestic, commercial, and industrial applications. Our team of qualified and registered gas practitioners is dedicated to delivering high-quality workmanship, safe installations, and full compliance with all regulations.

We specialize in LPG and Natural Gas installations, system maintenance, and gas leak detection across a wide range of environments — from single homes to commercial kitchens, housing developments, factories, and more.

We also provide services for industrial gases including Nitrogen, CO₂, and Argon, supporting industries such as manufacturing, powder coating, and food production.

At DP ENERGIES, we understand the importance of keeping your systems running safely and efficiently. That's why we offer comprehensive maintenance services to prevent downtime, ensure compliance, and extend the life of your gas infrastructure.

DP ENERGIES is your trusted partner for complete gas and energy solutions, including ongoing maintenance, emergency support, and compliance services. Whether you're planning a new build or maintaining existing infrastructure, we deliver solutions built on safety, reliability, and experience.

Certificate of Compliance (COC)

Understanding your gas system compliance requirements and how we ensure your safety.

What is a COC?

A Certificate of Compliance is a legal document confirming that your gas installation meets all safety standards and regulations.

When Do You Need One?

Required for new installations, major modifications, property sales, and insurance claims. Essential for legal compliance.

Our COC Process

Thorough inspection, pressure testing, safety verification, and official documentation by registered gas practitioners.

COC Services Include:

  • Complete system inspection
  • Pressure testing & leak detection
  • Official compliance documentation
  • Safety verification & recommendations
  • Fast turnaround times
  • Ongoing support & advice
