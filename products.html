Infiniti Premium Braai Solutions

Infiniti Fires represents a new generation in braais, combining rugged quality with stylish design and versatile cooking options.

Built-In Braais

Designed for permanent installation, these units offer an array of features and premium material choices (Mild Steel, 3CR12, Stainless Steel).

Combo Braais (Wood/Charcoal & Gas)

The ultimate choice for versatility. Combo Braais allow you to cook with the quick, convenient heat of gas or enjoy the authentic taste of wood/charcoal—or both at once for large parties!

Quick Cooking

Use the gas grill for quick, easy meals.

Authentic Flavour

Utilize the charcoal/wood section when you have time to make a fire.

Available Sizes: 1200mm & 1500mm versions.

Multifunction Braais (Wood/Charcoal)

Our most popular built-in sellers, the Multifunction range offers the classic braai experience with a host of customisable features.

Sizes: Available in 4 different sizes (e.g., 700, 1000, 1200, 1500).

Standard Features: Include a slide-in door, ember maker, and ember tray.

Optional Extras: Customise your unit with a 220V Chicken Spit & Motor, various Spit Baskets, Warming Grids, and more.

Freestanding & Wall Mounted Braais

Perfect for clients who do not have a brick chimney, these units offer a stylish, high-quality braai without the expense and disruption of major building work.

Easy Installation

Typically takes just 2 to 4 hours.

Portability

You can easily take this braai with you if you move.

Formats Available

Counter Top, Wall Mounted, Pedestal Storage, and Cabinet Storage.

Durability

The body, flue, and internal components are coated in heat and water-resistant paint, ideal for exposure to wet conditions.

Standard Features: Variable grid/souvla heights, ember maker, and an ash tray.
